Information about the procject

This story is created to spread awareness of Global Warming, based off of the UN's sustainable development goal 13: "Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts". By spreading awareness we are taking action, and through animations we are showing some of the consequences climate change will have on our earth.

How it works

This is a scrollytelling story being told as you scroll down the page. As you go down the page, pause at the different frames for informational text and watch the animations before scrolling further. The frames displays the different consequences climate change has on the different seasons.

Global Warming...

..is a real issue affecting the entire globe.

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's climate system observed since the pre-industrial period due to human activities, primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat-trapping greenhouse gas levels in Earth's atmosphere.

We will take you through every season and their changes

Shifting seasons are directly linked to warmer global temperatures. A slight change in temperature is enough to push the spring thaw earlier, and delay the first frost until later in the fall.

Summer

The summers are growing hotter.

The temperature is getting higher with an increased risk of drought. Warmer summers will also increase the intensity of heat waves and wildfires and would be a further source of air pollution.

The summer will also last longer which shortens the other seasons. Global warming will contribute with more frequent heat waves in coming years. Lack of water, lack of crops and unbearable heat will just be few of the issues we will have to face.

Fall

The fall season will have more extreme and destructive weather changes..

The warm temperature changes causes more destructive and extreme weather; when the ocean water heats up it provides more energy that in turn fuels the storms. Hurricanes have gotten stronger, and will continue to get stronger, due to climate change.

Not only will there be stronger and more frequent hurricanes; the moisture also attracts thunderstorms which will also become more severe. This will impact human life, the animal kingdom, cities and nature all the same.

Winter

The winters are getting shorter due to the increase of heat, which causes ice and permafrost to melt.

Ice protects the earth and oceans by reflecting excess heat back into space. This keeps the planet cooler; however, due to global warming, glaciers have begun to melt. As the ice melts sea levels rise, impacting coastal communities heavily. Wildlife is also threatened, with species such as walruses and polar bears losing their homes.

The permafrost will also begin to thaw, and this will have fatal concequences for the globe. When the permafrost thaws it leaves behind water and soil containing ancient bacteria and viruses. This can make humans and animals severely sick. It also releases greenhouse gases which further speeds up the process of global warming.
